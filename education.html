Food Safety Education

Learning about food safety is essential for both food handlers and consumers. It helps prevent foodborne illnesses and ensures proper food hygiene.

Tips for Safe Food Handling

  • Wash hands and surfaces often.
  • Cook food to the right temperature.
  • Refrigerate perishables promptly.
  • Separate raw and cooked foods.

Common Violations to Watch For

Improper storage, dirty kitchens, unclean uniforms, and lack of licenses are all red flags in food establishments.
