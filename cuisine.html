Explore Local Delicacies

Our city is known for a variety of traditional dishes that are both flavorful and nutritious. Here are a few you must try:

  • Hyderabadi Biryani – A rich blend of rice, spices, and marinated meat.
  • Mirchi ka Salan – A spicy chili curry made with groundnuts and sesame.
  • Double ka Meetha – A bread-based sweet dish soaked in milk and dry fruits.

Eat Local, Stay Safe

While exploring street food and local delicacies, ensure they are served hot, fresh, and in a clean environment.
